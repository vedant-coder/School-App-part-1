Dr. Vithalrao Vikhe Patil Foundation's English Medium School

HISTORY : The idea began to spread that childhood should be a time for learning, and schools for children were developed as places of learning.

SCHOOL VISION : To cater to the educational needs of children of transferable Government employees including defense and paramilitary personnel by providing a common programme of education.

STREAMS OFFERED : Science,Art and Commerce.

ACTIVITIES PERFORMED IN SCHOOL : Dancing, drawing, coloring etc.
